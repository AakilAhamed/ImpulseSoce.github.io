ABOUT

Impulse is a student run computer society of Anna University BIT Campus , Trichy. It was formed in the year 2022 by students who are passionate about Technology. Impulse is a community where we help each other to grow because the best things in life are always shared. The main goal of our community is 'Learn Together, Grow Together'. Impulse is a club of students by the students for the students.
